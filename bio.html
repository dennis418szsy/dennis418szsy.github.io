Biography

Borned in Guangzhou in 1992, I grow up in Shenzhen. When I was 18, I took the college entrance exam in Guangdong Province. Then, I went to Guangzhou and entered college, Sun Yat-Sen University. I spent 4 years in SYSU and got B.S. in mathematics and applied mathematics. In my senior year, I applied for graduate school and got the master admission from the statistics department at University of Washington. In the autumn of 2014, I came to Seattle and started my graduate study.
