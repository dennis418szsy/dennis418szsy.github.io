Biography

Born in Guangzhou in 1992, I grew up in Shenzhen until I went to college. When I was 18, I took the college entrance exam in Guangdong Province. Then, I went back to Guangzhou for undergraduate study. I spent 4 years in Sun Yat-Sen University and got my bachelor degree in mathematics and applied mathematics. In my senior year, I applied for graduate school and got the master admission from the statistics department at University of Washington. In the autumn of 2014, I came to Seattle and started my graduate study.
